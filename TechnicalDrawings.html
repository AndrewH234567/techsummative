Conclusion

In conclusion, Onshape has helped me communicate most of my drawings to you through Classroom, making my ideas easier to show to others so that they can get a clear picture of what I'm trying to do.

Onshape, CAD, and VCarve have all made it so easy to design what I'm thinking, and they're all much more exact than any drawing I would have made on paper, which is extremely useful because you can see exactly what you're going to make.

These technologies have also made it extremely easy to just click export and send a file to the laser cutter, CNC machine, 3D printer, etc., which definitely wouldn't be possible without them.
